MindKit App – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At ZenPath Studios, your privacy is a priority. MindKit is designed as a personal wellness tool that gives users full control over their data. Below is a clear explanation of what we collect, how it’s used, and how you remain in control.

1. Account Login & User Data

MindKit offers optional account creation using a valid email address. This allows you to:

  • Sync and back up your wellness data across devices
  • Restore your data if the app is deleted or your device is changed

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address (for authentication and sync)
  • Journal entries, wellness logs, custom prompts, and app preferences you choose to save

This data is securely stored in Google Firebase, a trusted cloud service. We do not access or share your data outside of providing sync functionality.

2. Local-Only Use (No Account)

If you use MindKit without logging in:

  • All your data remains local to your device
  • No journal entries, stats, or prompts leave your device
  • Deleting the app deletes all locally stored data

3. Ads and Third-Party Tools

MindKit uses Google AdMob to display banner ads in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect non-personal data such as:

  • Device type, OS, and language
  • Advertising ID
  • Interaction with ads

We do not collect or store this data, and it is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and AdMob policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

MindKit is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app with an account, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Use the app without ever signing in
  • Delete your account and all synced data by contacting us at https://zenpathstudios.com/contact.html

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as features change. We will post the latest version within the app and/or on our website. Continued use of MindKit after any update indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
