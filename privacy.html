Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how ZenPath (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website, apps, and services.

How We Use Information

We don’t sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., email or form processing) to operate our services.

Your Choices

Children

Our site and services aren’t directed to children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The revised date will be noted below.
